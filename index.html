Definition of Learning

Improving (performance measure P) with experience E at some task T

Play Go/Chess/Checkers

  • Task T: play according to the rules of the game
  • Performance meausre P: Percentage of games won
  • Experience E: play against yourself

Alvinn - self-driving car (Pommerleau 1992)

  • Task T: drive in a loop on the parking lot
  • Performance measure P: Sum of the squared differences to the human drivers steering angle
  • Experience E: human drivers

ML Applications: Loan Applications

If the bank loans money to someone who goes bankcrupt, then the bank will loose money

90% covered by simple tests for income and liabilities

Borderline cases are sent to a case officer. 50% of accepted borderline cases default on the loan

Simplest solution: reject all borderline cases. But the bank makes most money on borderline cases (high interest rates, late fees, ...)

ML Applications: Loan Applications

ML researchers implemented a Neural Network, that was 80% accurate

Bank actually uses a set of rules found by a rule induction system. Only 66% accurate

Bank prefered the rule-based system, since

  1. rules can be interpreted and verified by human
  2. rules can be explained to the customers. Customer that get rejected for a loan are angry at case officer and bank. Now the "party" responsible for the rejection is the computer program

ML Applications: Loan Applications

Task T: Accept or reject loan applications

Performance Measure P: Maximize profit for the bank including defaults, late fees, high interest rates, ...

Experience E: Outcome of prior loan applications and their associated data

ML Applications: Call Center Scheduling for Electricity Company

Task T: Estimate the number of staff needed in the call center of a utlity company

Experience E: Previous logs (Day of the week, billing data, weather (wind, rain), plant operation (power outages, loads), customer relations (new offer, new service, ...)

Performance Measure P: Weighted sum of money spent and customer wait times

ML Applications: Call Center Scheduling for Electricity Company

System found very simple rule:

Call center staff is proprotional to the number of people you disconnected from power (pink slips) you sent out

In hindsight, this makes sense. People only call if you shut off their power

Electricity company refused to pay, because they expected some complex model of the relationship

Generalization as Search

Avoiding Search is the Crux of AI

Inductive learning of classifiers (When to send special offer to customer)

  • Find a set of rules that fits the data

The set of rules define a language `(if last_purchase > 3 month ) and ( age < 50 ) and ...)`

Search: search through all possible rules (hypothesis space) to find ones that match the data

Generalization as Search

Trying to train a deep artifical neural network (DL)

Hypothesis space: GPT-3 [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GPT-3] 175 Billion floating point numbers (weights) and the architecture of the network

Backpropagation algorithm (software) and GPUs (hardware) can efficiently find the best set of weights for a given network architecture
